RoveCrest

Multidisciplinary team building a Mars Rover

We at RoveCrest are an engineering design team at University of California Davis building a Mars Rover to compete at the annual University Rover Challenge. Founded in the academic year of 2019 - 2020 we succesfully cleared the first stage of the competition, and going forward we aim to build a robust Rover that can not only win the competition but contribute to Humanity's space effort.
